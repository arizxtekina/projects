About Me

The person
behind the work

Hello, I'm Aniket

I'm a second-year student passionate about the intersection of technology and visual storytelling. My work spans graphic design, video editing, VFX, and motion graphics — disciplines that let me tell stories through both still and moving images.

Whether it's a crisp poster layout, a composited visual effect, or an animated sequence, I bring the same level of attention to detail and creative ambition. I believe every project is an opportunity to push what's visually possible.

I'm currently studying web development as part of my Semester 2 curriculum, adding a new dimension to my creative toolkit. Outside of screens, I'm constantly exploring new techniques and drawing inspiration from cinema, street art, and the ever-evolving world of digital media.
